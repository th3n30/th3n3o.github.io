    DiscoverOur Story

    Welcome To Casa Della Pizza!

    We are the brand new, and the first of our kind - an authentic Neapolitan pizzeria in a quaint corner of Madurai. Our objective is to bring our patrons the experience of original woodfired Pizza the Italians rave, and are proud about. A humble quest to bring a slice of authenticity and passion of Napoli into our hometown.

    We offer you the classic Neapolitan pizzas, and a range of speciality coffee and milkshakes/ mocktails to keep you entertained from starter to dessert. Our cooking process checks every box of the age old tradition of Neapolitan pizza making. We strive our best to bring you the high quality ingredients. From 00 flour, San Marzano tomatoes, locally produced fresh mozzarella cheese, extra virgin olive oil, to wide variety of imported cold cuts (chicken, pork & lamb)procured and cooked to perfection for you to taste the best variety of pizza to exist. We are located in a cosy corner that provides you with privacy and space of your own. A kid friendly place, open to gatherings and as well for families and couples to spend quality time together in a weekend. Come visit us and enjoy our tasteful art, or order through one of our delivery partners.
